Project Description:
Our project is a Deepfake detection system, that analyzes images and tells us if it's real or fake and also calculates the confidence score. It also gives us specific reasoning behind the result.

The Problem statement
In usual platforms, either the result is too basic, that is, it either tells us if it's fake or real or the platforms won't be available to the public. There is a growing need for an automated system that can analyze images and determine whether they are authentic or deepfake. Therefore, our product is useful.

The Solution
We are developing a Deepfake detection system with a pretrained CNN model which analyzes the image and determines whether real or fake. This also gives the confidence score and a human readable explanation which is really helpful non-technical users.
